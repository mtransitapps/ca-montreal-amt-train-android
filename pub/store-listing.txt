Full description:
This app adds Montreal RTM (AMT) trains information to MonTransit.

This app provides the planned schedule as well as real time service statuses and news from rtm.quebec, YouTube and @allo_exo, @exo4_CA, @exo6_DM, @exo2_SJ, @exo5_MA, @exo3_SH and @exo1_VH on Twitter.

RTM trains serve Montreal, Deux-Montagnes, Vaudreuil, Hudson, Saint-Jérôme and Mont-Saint-Hilaire in Quebec, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display trains information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the Réseau de Transport Métropolitain.
https://rtm.quebec/en/about/open-data

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-montreal-amt-train-android

This app is not related with the Réseau de Transport Métropolitain.

Permissions:
- Other: required for real-time service statuses and to read news from rtm.quebec, YouTube and Twitter
